About Items

We deal in almost 100+ types of items which are specifically based on Pickles(Achar), Chutney and Murabbay with different taste and varieties. We have further two different categories of pickles i.e pickles made with Deccan Hyderabadi Formula and pickles made with Shikarpuri Formula. All pickles are made in either oil with masala or in vinegar. Furthermore, we have all kinds of Murabbay which are sometimes very beneficial for the health in different cases, h Bahi Murabba is preferred by many doctors to the heart patients and Aamla Murabba to the people suffering from hair loss. In addition, we deal in all kinds of sweet and sour chutney that are very delicious to have as a part of meal. While, some of our other useful items includes different types of vinegars that are use in cooking purposes, the uncooked fryable or roastable snacks, and many more items to explore... Indeed all of our items are purely hygienic and made with high quality and clean ingredients.
